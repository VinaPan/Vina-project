  • Marvel Comics is an American comics giant. It was founded in 1939 and officially named Marvel in 1961. It was translated as "Amazing Manga" and used to be called "Timely Comics", Atlas Comics.

Marvel Cinematic Universe

  • The Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) is an aerial world and a common world of Marvel Film's series of films based on Marvel comic characters. The TV series further expands the Marvel movie universe.

  • Like the Marvel universe in comics, the common world is created by common elements, settings, performances, and characters through cross-border works, and is an officially recognized multiverse with other comics, movies, and animation series.
